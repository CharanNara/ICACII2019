An Efficient Intrusion Detection System with Convolutional Neural Network

V Maheshwar Reddy, I Ravi Prakash Reddy and K Adi Narayana Reddy

ACE ENGINEERING COLLEGE

ABSTRACT

Cyber security is the most challenging and risky aspects of the modern digital world. Due to the availability of computational resources, the area of Deep Learning is extensively used in many fields. In this proposal, we use Convolutional Neural Network for intrusion detection. Intrusion detection system with Convolutional Neural Networks helps us to detect, analyze, and categorize the incoming or outgoing traffic into normal or attack. In this paper, we implemented IDS with CNN for binary classification and multiclass classification on given traffic data.. The results show that the proposed IDS using CNN is better than the other existing IDS models using machine learning.
