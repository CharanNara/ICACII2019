Oral Presentations

Predictive & Interactive IOT Diagnosis system
with AI and ML Tools: Review

Pavitra Busupalli, D.Narendar Singh and Prof.Sudhir Kumar Sharma

Al Musanna College of Technology, Oman

ABSTRACT

Alzmeir Disease is common and tremendous growing neuro based disease over the globe. Several Biomedical devices for detection and prediction of Alzheimer disease with IOT based remote monitoring system were developed. For optimization and accurate Alzheimer Disease prediction a new wearable IOT frame work is designed and developed by integrating EEG(Electroencephalogram )and EMG(Electromyograph) sensors with interfacing Machine Learning and Neural Network Algorithms.Data collected from sensors and applied different Algorithm to analyze the data to diagnosis the Alzheimer Disease, Collected EEG and EMG sensor i.e Electrodes connected to the motor cortex portion of brain scalp with the same resembling points connected with EMG electrodes to muscular points of the body as a wearable device is processed through Arduino and pushed the same data to cloud server with Raspberri-Pi node. Collected data is compared with waveform parameters of Alpha,Beta,Theta and Delta. Artificial intelligence and Neural Network Algorithm were applied for Diagnosis of Alzmeir Disease,the obtained result is shared with Patient, Patients care taker and doctor through designed Interactive IOT system to avoid and rescue the patient to escalate next stage of disease.
